Fleeting Blossom



Digital Composition Print 22 x 28 inches, 2019

This piece is inspired by my roots, my hometown is in the middle of the almond orchards of the central valley. In February, the blossoms arrive for a very short but overwhelming performance. They are a reminder that life is short, changing, beautiful and symbiotic.

This is a compliation of photographs of blossoms I took myself, layered on top of a close up photograph of the almond tree bark, layered with a watercolor stain. All of these layers intermingled with gesture lines.
