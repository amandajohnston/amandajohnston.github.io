Social Media Glitch Art



Glitch Art, 2019

Social Media Glitch Art is an expression of my interaction with social media distorted by creative force. This work is a compilation of four glitch art pieces created using audio editing software on a jpeg file. It is exciting in that the result imposed is entirely unpredictable yet still aesthetically pleasing and poignant.
