Amanda Johnston is a California based artist currently studying Pictorial Art at San Jose State University. Her work is currently an evolution of exploration to portray meaningful visual concepts. She enjoys both drawing illogical relationships between visual components such as ballerinas and bourbon and creating ideological works of significant social and personal themes. She plans to earn a Master of Fine Arts degree to become a professional artist and art educator.
